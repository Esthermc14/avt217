Draw an ice cream cone!

Instructions

  • 1. Open up XD on your computer
  • 2. Click "New File"
  • 3. Adjust the size of your board on the transform bar on the right side of your screen to make the width= 1440 and the heigth=1080 and continue with the default settings.
  • 4. Now that your board is set up select the polygon icon on the left bar and draw a triangle with a width=297 and a height=393
  • Select the triangle and hover over one of the corners to flip it upside down.
  • Now select ellipse icon on the left bar to draw three circles of the same size (W=265 and H=249)
  • Place the ice cream balls on top of the cone and place to next to each other and one on the top.
  • Send the circle on the left to the back and then the top circle to the back of those two.
  • Now draw a smaller circle. This wil be the cherry. The dimension for this circle are W=49 and H=42
  • Enjoy!
